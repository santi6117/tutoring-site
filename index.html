Who I Am

About Me 🎓

Hello! I'm Santi — a 4.0 GPA OU Math graduate with an AI minor, and incoming MSc student at the University of Oxford this Fall. With 3+ years of tutoring experience, I've worked with organizations and private students alike to help dozens of math students excel in math.

My approach is personal and relational — every student starts with a diagnostic, helping me understand exactly where they’re at, what they need, and how they learn best. From there, I use my background in high-level mathematics to break down complex problems and help students understand the why behind a problem, not just the steps. That is what builds understanding that really sticks.

I believe every student has the ability to succeed — sometimes a small push is all they need. By tutoring your student, I want to leave them not only with good grades, but with a genuine confidence in themselves and maybe even a curiosity for the subject. I’m here to help — if you have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out.
